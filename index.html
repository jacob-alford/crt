Complex Reaction Time v3.0

This applet improves on Complex Reaction Time v2.0. Previously, you had to match the color of text with a colored circle. The complexity arrised from distinguishing between the color that the text said and the color of the text.

This applet is inspired by a research test within 23andMe. Basically, the applet will show you one of nine symbols. Your job is to click 1,2, or 3 by matching the symbol to the list of symbols. It is scored by total answered and accuracy.

How to play

  1. Select the difficulty you wish to play at. The symbols displayed in the Current Symbols section will be the key to matching the symbols.
  2. Press play when you are ready.
  3. There will be 50 total rounds.
  4. A random symbol from the list will be displayed, press the number corresponding to the displayed symbol on your keyboard (1,2,3).
  5. If you wish, you can submit your score to my database anonymously for research purposes. Your score, the difficulty, the time taken, the current time, and IP will be recorded.
